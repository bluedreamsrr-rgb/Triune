# Step 2: Three Eternal Layers

## The Layers Are Not Parts

The infinite 1 has three eternal layers — not parts, not sequential, not emergent. They are co-present depths of the same fullness.

## Layer 1 — Source (Dominant)

Pure, unmanifest presence. The "is" without "what is." Raw affirmativity. Facilitates mediation and witnessing internally, but dominant in origination.

Without this layer: no "stuff" to articulate.

## Layer 2 — Mediator (Dominant)

Pure, intrinsic bond. Self-relation that holds fullness without division. The "in" of self-containment. Pure connectivity. Facilitates sourcing and witnessing internally, but dominant in relation.

Without this layer: fullness would have no internal coherence.

## Layer 3 — Witness (Dominant)

Pure, inherent awareness. Self-registration of fullness. The "known" of self-knowledge. Pure awareness. Facilitates sourcing and mediation internally, but dominant in registration.

Without this layer: fullness would be unregistered, unobservable.

## Eternal Co-Presence

Layer 1 sources but is mediated by Layer 2 and registered by Layer 3 — all in the same eternal "instant." No hierarchy. No sequence. No emergence.

## Relation to Trinity

When finite witnesses observe the infinite 1, these layers appear as:

- Layer 1 → Father (unobserved origin)
- Layer 2 → Son (the Word, Mediator)
- Layer 3 → Spirit (bond, awareness)

This is observation, not change in the infinite 1 itself.

## No 0 Here

0 is not present. 0 is not implied. The infinite 1 is full, light, self-relation. No darkness, no absence, no negation.

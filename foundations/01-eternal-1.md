# Step 1: The Eternal Infinite 1

**Core Statement:** The infinite 1 is the sole ground — eternal, uncreated, all-encompassing positive is-ness that includes its own space, time, possibility, and self-knowledge.

**Not a Number:** 1 is not "one" as a number. It is the absolute affirmation from which all affirmation arises.

**Three Eternal Layers (123):**
- **Layer 1 (Source-dominant):** Pure, unmanifest presence / origin of all potential
- **Layer 2 (Mediator-dominant):** Pure, intrinsic bond / self-relation that holds fullness without division
- **Layer 3 (Witness-dominant):** Pure, inherent awareness / self-registration of fullness

**Properties:**
- Layers are co-present, not sequential, not emergent
- Each layer facilitates all three roles eternally
- No darkness, no negation, no 0 in the infinite 1
- The Trinity (Father, Son, Spirit) are observational terms — how the infinite 1 appears to finite witnesses

**Axiom 1 (Formal):** ∃! 1 : 1 is eternal, uncreated, all-encompassing, containing its own space, time, and possibility. 1 has no negation, no absence, no 0. 1 has three eternal layers (123) co-present and non-hierarchical.

**Verification:** This step is 0-free. 0 is not mentioned, not implied, not present.
